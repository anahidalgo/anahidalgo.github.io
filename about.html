About

Who am I?

Hello! I'm Ana. I was born in the city of Rio de Janeiro. My family comes from Peru, but I am the first Brazilian in the family! Thanks to my origins, I learned to speak Spanish at the same time that I learned to speak Portuguese.

I have a degree in Architecture from the Federal University of Rio de Janeiro (UFRJ), and I carry with me the strong social awareness that I learned in the course. Planning and designing environments is an activity that I do with great pleasure, but drawing has always been my greatest passion.

Since I was a child I like to draw, and when I became an adult, I turned my passion into my profession. Architecture gives me a way to represent the world through drawing. And, uniting with civil construction, I tried to help people with my work.

However, the desire to represent realities never left me, so I continued my studies in illustration. With the awareness of the world that I learned to have in the Architecture course, I started to apply them in my drawings.

I work with patterns, using my dear paper and pencil, and I also work with the digital world and its trends. With that, I also acquired experience in the editorial and advertising line.

Today I share my drawing board of plants, cuts and facades, with stories of stories, dreams and people. I seek to unite the two experiences, from reality to fantasy, to satisfy customers, always with professionalism and great affection.
